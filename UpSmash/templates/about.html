About

What is UpSmash

UpSmash is a project dedicated to bringing you the best stats possible from Super Smash Bros Melee. This includes a variety of sources, including Slippi ranked data, Slippi files, and comparisons to the broader community.

Who works on UpSmash

Chris Schmitt

Has been a member of the Melee community since 2015. Been working on passion projects around Melee since 2018 and started working on a Slippi file database in late-2022.

Ryan Yueing

A newer member of the community, Ryan has been instrumental the start of UpSmash, including planning, working on backend tasks, and database layouts.
